Help, I Can't Open the eBooks!

The is in ZIP format. You will need a program such as WinRar or WinZip to extract the files.

The eBooks themselves are in PDF format. You will need a PDF reader such as Adobe to open them.


Links:

WinRar - Open RAR and ZIP files
WinZip - Open ZIP files
Adobe PDF - Open PDF files

WinZip and WinRar are completely free to use and safe to install on your system. They are file archivers, meaning they group together files and compress them, to reduce their file size. Archivers make it easy for people to transfer groups of files to one another.

Once you've downloaded and installed the software, you can now extract the zip file. Right click on the file and click "Extract to download\NeobuxUltimateStrategy\"

After that, a new folder will be created where the ebook files are. Open the folder, and the ebooks will be in there.
