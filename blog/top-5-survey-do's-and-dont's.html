Don't get stuck on any one survey program

If you find yourself often being disqualified, or receiving surveys that are low paying, you're probably not in the demographic for that particular survey. Instead of being frustrated, just move on. Don't fall for programs that promise to pay big

Some programs say they pay $75 or more for a 5 minute survey, but there's no way it can be true. If it sounds too good to be true, then it probably is. You'll just end up wasting your time. Be realistic and only take surveys that are legit.

Don't pay for anything in hopes of larger reward

There are many of what's called the "free trial" scam. These trials are not free at all, they promise to pay you $50 to try a service like Netflix. Offers like this are sometimes full of stipulations such as you have to stay subscribed for one years. Many people end up losing money rather than making money on these free trial offers.

While it's easy to sign up for a program that may only ask for your name and email address, you'll find that many of these operations are a waste of time.
