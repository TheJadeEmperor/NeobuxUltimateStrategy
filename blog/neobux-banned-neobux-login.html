How to Reset Your IP Address

After you clear your cookies, the next step is to reset your IP address. Once you do this, you will assume a new identity, as far as Neobux is concerned. They have no way of knowing that it's you.

You can renew your IP address by cycling your modem's power. Turn off both your computer and your modem (and your router, if you have one). Wait at least 5 minutes, then turn everything back on. If your computer is set to receive an IP address dynamically (the most likely configuration), you should be assigned a new IP address automatically.

To reset your IP on a windows computer...

1. Open the command prompt window. Do this by clicking the "Start" button, and then selecting "Run."

2. In the window that appears, type "cmd" into the text box and click the "OK" button. 3. Release the computer's current IP address. To do this, type "ipconfig /release" into the command prompt window and press Enter.

4. Acquire a new IP address. Do this by typing "ipconfig /renew" into the command prompt window and pressing Enter. This should assign the computer a new IP address, which will be different from the previous address.

Check to make sure your IP address has changed. To do this, go to this website: https://www.whatsmyip.org/

It is a good idea to check your IP address BEFORE these steps, so you can check that your IP has changed afterwards.

Finally, it's time to make a new Neobux login with a brand new email address. You should be able to login to your new account and use it as you did before. See? I told you not to panic :)
